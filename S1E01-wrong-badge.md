# 🏢 S1E01 · 打招呼与回应
共享办公楼前台｜被误认为活动嘉宾
Player ♂/♀ · Receptionist ♀ · 全部西方人 · POV 第一人称
Level: A0 / Pre-A1

---

## 🎬 镜头规则（强制）
- ⚠️ **SHOT 1 为独立镜头**（仅 Receptionist 开场白），三条路线共用，文件中仅出现一次。
- ⚠️ **SHOT 2 起，每个 SHOT = Player 先说 → Receptionist 回应，一来一回为一个镜头。禁止跨 SHOT 合并。**
- LIBTV 分镜时：SHOT 数量 = 独立镜头数量，逐 SHOT 输出，不允许压缩或跳号。

---

## 🔀 对话结构说明
SHOT 1 共享开场。SHOT 2 起分叉为 A / B / C。
- **路线 A**：正确回应，任务完成，沟通顺畅
- **路线 B**：听错 / 不回应，拿错物品，轻微社交翻车
- **路线 C**：犹豫含糊后被推着走，被送去完全不对的地方

---

## 📋 目标语言点
| 功能 | 目标短语 |
|------|---------|
| 问候 | Hi. / Hello. / Good morning. / How are you? |
| 回应 | I'm good. / Fine, thanks. / I'm here. |
| 确认 | Can you hear me? / Yes. / ...Hello? |
| 拒绝 | No. / I am not... |
| 感谢 | Thank you. / No problem. |
| 道歉 | Sorry. / I'm sorry. |
| 告别 | See you. / Bye. / Have a good day. |
| 核心名词 | morning, meeting, friend, day |
| 核心动词 | meet, hear, see, say |

*（她从高脚椅上站起来，拿起胸牌，笑容明亮）*

## 路线 A · 正确回应 → ✅
欧美真人出镜，全程第一人称 POV 视角，明亮暖调美式

### SHOT 2 ⬡ A-ONLY
**Player:** Oh, hi. I'm good — but I am not here for the meetup.
**Receptionist:** Oh? Something is wrong?
*（她停住动作，胸牌悬在半空，眉毛微抬）*

### SHOT 3 ⬡ A-ONLY
**Player:** I have a meeting. With Mr. Zhang. I'm here for a meeting.
**Receptionist:** Oh! I'm sorry. I made a mistake.
*（她把胸牌放回台面，表情从惊讶转为不好意思的微笑）*

### SHOT 4 ⬡ A-ONLY
**Player:** No problem. Thank you.
**Receptionist:** Mr. Zhang is here. Floor 12, room 1208. The elevator is over there.
*（她伸手指向电梯方向，笑容回暖）*

### SHOT 5 ⬡ A-ONLY
**Player:** Great. Thank you. Bye!
**Receptionist:** Have a good day!
*（她挥手告别，笑得温暖）*

✅ 正确回应，任务完成，沟通顺畅

### 🎨 Route A Storyboard Prompt
Comic storyboard. 6 panels. First-person POV. Western female receptionist, late 20s, warm brown hair in a loose bun, cream blouse. Modern co-working lobby, polished concrete floors, warm wood desk, floor-to-ceiling windows, golden afternoon sun. Color palette: creamy beige, warm amber, soft sage green. Subtle bloom on highlights. 16:9.

Panel 1 【Warm Welcome】⬡ SHARED-ALL — Medium shot. Receptionist rising from high stool behind curved wooden desk, badge lifted toward camera. Sunlight catches badge sleeve, tiny lens flare. Genuine bright smile reaching her eyes. Living wall of plants backdrop. Shallow depth of field. SHOT 1.

Panel 2 【Polite Correction】⬡ A-ONLY — Medium shot. Receptionist pauses mid-action, badge frozen in air. Her eyebrows lift, lips part — curious, not offended. The badge dangles from her fingertips in a beam of afternoon light. SHOT 2.

Panel 3 【Apologetic Smile】⬡ A-ONLY — Close-up. Badge now placed down on desk (foreground blur). Receptionist gives a small apologetic smile — the kind where someone feels a little silly but handles it gracefully. Warm light on her face. SHOT 3.

Panel 4 【Helpful Redirection】⬡ A-ONLY — Wide shot. Receptionist stepped out from behind desk, arm extended pointing toward glass elevator bank. She looks back over her shoulder, smiling warmly. Lobby stretches behind her. Composition leads eye: hand → elevator → up. SHOT 4.

Panel 5 【Fond Farewell】⬡ A-ONLY — Medium close-up. Receptionist leaning forward on desk, chin lifted, waving with wiggling fingers. Golden hour light floods frame in honey tones. SHOT 5.

Panel 6 【✅ Ending Card】⬡ A-ONLY — Wide shot from behind. Protagonist's silhouette approaching sunlit elevator doors. Receptionist small in background at desk, still waving. ✅ overlay bottom right.

## 路线 B · 听错/不回应 → ❌
欧美真人出镜，全程第一人称 POV 视角，冷调

### SHOT 2 ⬡ B-ONLY
**Player:** ...Sorry?
*（你没听清，站在原地没有动）*
**Receptionist:** Hello? Can you hear me?
*（她的笑容消退，身体前倾，语气困惑）*

### SHOT 3 ⬡ B-ONLY
**Player:** I... yes. Here. I am here.
*（你说得含糊，手指了指自己，又指了指里面）*
**Receptionist:** I do not understand. Sorry.
*（她放下胸牌，皱眉）*

### SHOT 4 ⬡ B-ONLY
**Player:** ...Meeting?
**Receptionist:** Yes — the meetup. The meeting. Here is your badge.
*（她不太确定，但还是把胸牌递了过来）*

### SHOT 5 ⬡ B-ONLY
**Player:** *（你看胸牌：「VISITOR - Tech Meetup」。这不是你的会议。你想说什么——）*
**Receptionist:** The meeting room is through those doors.
*（她已转身接电话，不再看你）*

### SHOT 6 ⬡ B-ONLY
**Player:** *（你拿着错的胸牌站在原地。电梯在右边，但活动门在正前方——你已经走错了方向）*
**Receptionist:** *（她的声音从背后传来，在电话里说着什么，你已不可及）*

❌ 听错关键词，拿错物品，任务失败

### 🎨 Route B Storyboard Prompt
Comic storyboard. 6 panels. First-person POV. Same receptionist, same lobby. Color palette starts warm then cools: golden → desaturated taupes → muted gray-blues. Lighting shifts from afternoon sun to flat fluorescent. 16:9.

Panel 1 【Same Opening】⬡ SHARED-ALL — Medium shot. Identical to Route A Panel 1 — receptionist rising, badge lifted, warm smile, golden light. The false start that makes the unraveling more painful. SHOT 1.

Panel 2 【The Confusion】⬡ B-ONLY — Medium shot. Receptionist's smile has faded into a puzzled expression. She leans forward over the desk, neck craned, trying to parse the mumbling. Badge now lowered to chest. Her eyes search the space where "I" would be standing. SHOT 2.

Panel 3 【Lost in Translation】⬡ B-ONLY — Close-up on receptionist's face. Brows furrowed, lips slightly parted — genuine confusion, not anger. Badge fully down on desk now, forgotten. She's trying to understand but can't. SHOT 3.

Panel 4 【The Wrong Handoff】⬡ B-ONLY — POV of protagonist's hand reaching forward. The badge is sharp in focus: "VISITOR - Tech Meetup" clearly legible. The receptionist's hand is at frame edge, having just released it. Her expression behind it: uncertain but moving on. SHOT 4.

Panel 5 【Too Late】⬡ B-ONLY — Medium shot. Protagonist's hands holding the wrong badge at chest level. Beyond, receptionist has turned away, phone to ear, body language closed. A door marked "MEETUP →" is visible. The window to correct has closed. SHOT 5.

Panel 6 【❌ Wrong Place】⬡ B-ONLY — Overhead high angle. Protagonist standing alone in the lobby, wrong badge in hand, caught between the meetup door ahead and the elevator to the right — already funneled down the wrong path. Receptionist visible in background, tiny figure still on phone. Harsh fluorescent, flattened lighting. ❌ overlay bottom right.
