# 🏢 Wrong Badge
你走进共享办公楼见客户，前台把你当成了活动嘉宾。
Player ♂/♀ · Receptionist ♀ · 全部西方人 · POV 第一人称

---

## 🎬 镜头规则（强制）
- ⚠️ **SHOT 1 为独立镜头**（仅 Receptionist 开场白），三条路线共用，文件中仅出现一次。
- ⚠️ **SHOT 2 起，每个 SHOT = Player 先说 → Receptionist 回应，一来一回为一个镜头。禁止跨 SHOT 合并。**
- LIBTV 分镜时：SHOT 数量 = 独立镜头数量，逐 SHOT 输出，不允许压缩或跳号。

---

## 🔀 对话结构说明
三条路线共享 SHOT 1（开场）。SHOT 2 起分叉为 A / B / C。
路线 A 和 B 在中间 SHOT 上结构对应（查平板、给房间号），但语气不同。
路线 C 在 SHOT 4 设有 **分叉点**：此时玩家若纠正「No, I have a meeting」，可切回路线 A 或 B。

### 🎨 Route A Storyboard Prompt
Comic storyboard. 6 panels. First-person POV. Western female receptionist, late 20s, warm brown hair in a loose bun, cream blouse. Modern co-working lobby, polished concrete floors, warm wood desk, floor-to-ceiling windows, golden afternoon sun. Color palette: creamy beige, warm amber, soft sage green. Subtle bloom on highlights. 16:9.

Panel 1 【Warm Welcome】⬡ SHARED-ALL — Medium shot. Receptionist rising from high stool behind curved wooden desk, leaning forward, badge lifted toward camera. Sunlight catches badge sleeve creating lens flare. Genuine bright smile reaching her eyes. Living wall of plants backdrop. Shallow depth of field. This is SHOT 1 — the only standalone shot, shared by all routes.

Panel 2 【Polite Surprise】⬡ A-ONLY — Close-up. Receptionist's face and badge frozen mid-air. Eyebrows lift, lips part — curious not offended. Badge dangles from fingertips in a ray of light, casting soft shadow on her blouse. Expression says "wait, what?" with warmth. Soft rim light outlines hair.

Panel 3 【Apologetic Recovery】⬡ SHARED-AB — Medium shot, low angle. Badge now on desk (foreground blur). Receptionist holds tablet, head tilted down, faint apologetic half-smile. Tablet casts cool blue light up on her face, contrasting with warm sun from windows. Warm and cool light duel.

Panel 4 【Helpful Redirection】⬡ SHARED-AB — Wide shot. Receptionist stepped out from behind desk, arm extended pointing toward glass elevator bank. Looking back over shoulder at camera, smiling warmly. Lobby stretches behind her, soft afternoon shadows across floor. Eye line: hand → elevator → up.

Panel 5 【Fond Farewell】⬡ A-ONLY — Medium close-up. Receptionist leaning forward on desk, chin lifted, smiling broadly. Waving with wiggling fingers. Golden hour light floods frame in honey tones. Old-friends-parting warmth.

Panel 6 【🏢 Ending Card】⬡ A-ONLY — Wide shot from behind. Protagonist's silhouette approaching sunlit elevator doors. Receptionist small in background at desk, still waving. 🏢 overlay bottom right.

### 🎨 Route B Storyboard Prompt
Comic storyboard. 6 panels. First-person POV. Same receptionist, same lobby. Color palette cooled: desaturated taupes, steel blues, muted grays. Light flat — overcast or windows shaded. Lifted blacks, reduced saturation. 16:9.

Panel 1 【The Setup】⬡ SHARED-ALL — Medium shot. Structurally identical to Route A Panel 1 — receptionist rising from stool, badge in hand, warm smile. But light is colder, flatter. Badge catches fluorescent gleam instead of warm sun. Same frame, foreshadowing the chill to come. This is SHOT 1 — shared, but the mood already hints at what's coming.

Panel 2 【The Smile Dies】⬡ B-ONLY — Close-up. Receptionist's smile collapsed: mouth settled into neutral line, eyes flat and professional. Badge lowered to chest height, held loosely. Posture drawn back — tiny physical retreat. Overhead fluorescent casts slight shadows under brows. Blank professionalism: you're not worth the emotional energy.

Panel 3 【The Cold Screen】⬡ SHARED-AB — Medium shot, same composition as Route A Panel 3 — receptionist with tablet, face lit from below by blue-white glow. But here: zero eye contact, completely neutral expression. Badge discarded on desk corner out of focus. Shoulders slightly hunched. Same frame, opposite emotional register.

Panel 4 【Flat Directions】⬡ SHARED-AB — Medium shot. Receptionist looks up briefly from tablet, face blank. One hand gestures vaguely toward elevator without warmth. Eyes already returning to screen before finishing the sentence. Same composition as Route A Panel 4, all warmth drained.

Panel 5 【The Bureaucratic Wall】⬡ B-ONLY — Wide shot across reception desk. Thick sign-in ledger dominates lower half of frame, pen diagonally across empty line. Receptionist upper half, turned away in profile toward monitor. Physical distance between book and her feels like a chasm. Flat fluorescent light.

Panel 6 【😐 Cold Exit】⬡ B-ONLY — Wide shot toward elevator lobby. Protagonist's silhouette walks toward distant doors. Receptionist in extreme foreground, out of focus, profile turned away — looking at screen, refusing farewell glance. Harsh fluorescent pools on floor. 😐 overlay bottom right.
